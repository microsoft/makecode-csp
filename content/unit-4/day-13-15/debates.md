## Details

### 1. Debate 1 (25 minutes)

* Randomly select one of the debate topics.
* Determine who team 1 and team 2 are.
* Flip a coin:
    * Heads = Team 1 = for the proposition
    * Tails = Team 2 = against the proposition
* Conduct the debate for Topic 1.
